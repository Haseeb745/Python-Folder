005 : An EDI and SAP ABAP Developer

As part of the project at my work place worked as an EDI enabler and SAP ABAP developer

My Client(undisclosed for security reasons) has full capability to design, develop, manufacture and complete the functional modular interior systems for automobile be it for large scale or small scale. Their expertise in manufacturing materials, design and styling helps them to build smart interiors with modern and adaptable materials so that customers differentiate vehicles efficiently.

Hereby working as Supplier Enabler Lead in this project I dealt with all the Supplier Communication of my client. This work of mine includes all of the Supplier related works starting from the enablement of the Suppliers with my client to their successful business flow (i.e. make the business documents flow through EDI). Not just worked as Supplier Enabler here but also held different roles like Customer Enabler and also the off shore admin of the project and its tools like B2B Sterling Integrator, Dell Boomi, SAP PI/PO and other client specific tools which project made use of. Working as Abaper in this project I have held the responsibility to work with SAP Teams helping them with creating Smart Forms, Reports and also deal with other traits of SAP ABAP which project made use of.
